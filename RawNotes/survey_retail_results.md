**Retail Customer Crypto Integration Survey - Summary**

**Date:** February 2026

**Participants:** 2,500 active retail banking customers

**Methodology:** Email survey with in-app notification prompt.

---

**Headline Finding:**

A significant majority, **70% of respondents, expressed a desire for some form of cryptocurrency integration** within their existing mobile banking application.

---

**Key Breakdowns:**

*   **What features are most desired?**
    *   **Buy/Sell Crypto:** 65% of interested customers want the ability to buy and sell major cryptocurrencies (like Bitcoin and Ethereum) directly from their checking/savings accounts.
    *   **View Crypto Holdings:** 58% want to see their external crypto wallet balances (e.g., from Coinbase, MetaMask) inside the banking dashboard.
    *   **Crypto Rewards:** 45% were interested in credit/debit cards that offer crypto-back rewards instead of points or cash back.
    *   **Earn Yield:** 30% showed interest in "staking" or "earning" features on their crypto holdings.

*   **Demographics of "Interested" Respondents:**
    *   **Age:** The 25-40 age group showed the highest interest (85%). The 18-24 group was a close second (81%). Interest dropped to 40% for the 55+ age group.
    *   **Account Balance:** Interest was surprisingly consistent across all income/balance levels, suggesting this is not a niche request limited to HNW clients.

*   **Primary Concerns:**
    *   **Security:** 88% cited security as their biggest concern.
    *   **Complexity:** 75% are worried about the learning curve and "making a mistake."
    *   **Volatility:** 60% are concerned about price fluctuations.

**Conclusion:**

The data reveals a clear and widespread demand for crypto services among our retail customer base. The primary barrier to adoption is not a lack of interest, but fear and a desire for a trusted, secure, and simple user experience. Customers want to engage with this asset class through the institution they already trust. This presents a major strategic opportunity.
